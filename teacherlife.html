My Teacher Life

My teacher life began as a kindergarten teacher in Floyd County, Ky. I was there for 4 years before I came to Magoffin County where I worked at Herald Whitaker Middle School for 2 years as a 6th grade math teacher. I then moved to South Magoffin Elementary where I now teach and have been for the last 6 years. I teach 5th grade reading and math, as well as, 6th grade math.
